Princess Rescue

Instructions

Use the WASD keys to move the knight and then press the the E key when you are near her to save her!
The amount of times you save her are in the top right corner the timer to the left of it is a countdown clock
the time goes down by 100 milliseconds every time you save her.
The score to win for easy is 10 with a starting time of ten seconds
The score to win for medium is 20 with a time of 7 seconds
The score for hard is 30 with 5 seconds inbetween
